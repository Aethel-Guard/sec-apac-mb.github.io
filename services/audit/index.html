Compliance Audit

ISO 27001, SOC 2, GDPR, and NIST framework alignment. Gap analysis, policy documentation, and audit-ready evidence — without the consulting bloat.

Frameworks we support

  • ISO 27001:2022 — Information Security Management System audit, Annex A controls
  • SOC 2 Type I & II — Trust Services Criteria (Security, Availability, Confidentiality, Processing Integrity, Privacy)
  • GDPR — Data protection impact assessments, Article 32 technical measures, data processing records
  • NIST CSF 2.0 — Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, Recover framework alignment
  • NIST 800-53 — Control baseline for federal and regulated industries
  • PCI DSS 4.0 — Payment card data protection (for organizations handling cardholder data)
  • CIS Controls v8 — Practical hardening checklist mapped to your environment

Our approach

Phase 1: Gap Analysis

We compare your current security posture against the target framework. You get a heat map showing exactly where you stand — green, yellow, red per control.

Phase 2: Policy Documentation

Missing policies written from scratch. Existing policies reviewed and updated. We don't hand you templates — we write policies that match how your organization actually works.

Phase 3: Evidence Collection

Auditors want evidence, not claims. We help you collect and organize:

  • Access logs and review records
  • Vulnerability scan reports
  • Incident response documentation
  • Training records and attestations
  • Vendor risk assessments
  • Change management tickets

Phase 4: ARCZT Vault for Evidence

All audit evidence is stored in ARCZT Vault — zero-knowledge, encrypted, access-controlled. Auditors get time-limited sharing links. You revoke access when the audit closes. No shared Google Drive folders, no emailed spreadsheets.

📋 Audit-ready, not audit-scared
We deliver a complete evidence pack organized by control ID. Your auditor opens the pack, finds what they need, and closes the audit faster.

Engagement model

  • One-time gap analysis — Know where you stand, 1-2 weeks
  • Full compliance program — Gap to audit-ready, 8-16 weeks depending on framework
  • Annual maintenance — Keep evidence current, policies updated, controls validated
