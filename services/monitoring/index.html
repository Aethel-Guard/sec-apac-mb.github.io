Threat Monitoring

Continuous monitoring of your attack surface. Early warning for emerging threats, CVEs, and breach events — before they impact your organization.

What we monitor

  • CVE intelligence — New vulnerabilities in software your organization uses, scored by exploitability and exposure
  • Breach events — When your organization's domains or emails appear in new breach databases
  • Attack surface changes — New open ports, exposed services, DNS changes, SSL certificate expiry
  • Dark web mentions — Organization name, domains, and key personnel mentioned in breach forums and paste sites
  • Supply chain alerts — Vulnerabilities in your vendors' software, upstream dependency compromises
  • Threat actor activity — Campaigns targeting your industry or region
⚡ Early Warning
Get notified days or weeks before an attack hits your infrastructure. CVE exploitation often follows public disclosure — we close that window.

How it works

1. Baseline

We map your attack surface — external IPs, domains, software inventory, cloud services. This becomes the monitoring baseline.

2. Continuous scanning

Daily automated scans of your external attack surface. Weekly deep scans of exposed services. Real-time CVE feeds matched against your software inventory.

3. Alert delivery

Alerts delivered via:

  • Encrypted email (PGP or ARCZT Vault sharing)
  • Direct to Vigilar app (breach alerts appear in the Breach Monitor)
  • Slack/Teams webhook (if configured)
  • Phone call for critical severity (CVE with active exploitation + your exposure confirmed)

4. Vigilar integration

When we detect a breach affecting your organization, the alert flows directly to your team's Vigilar apps. Each user sees the breach in their Breach Monitor, and if they have ARCZT Vault, they can check which safe entries are linked — all on-device, all private.

Service tiers

  • Standard — Daily external scan, CVE matching, breach alerts. Weekly summary report.
  • Professional — All of Standard + dark web monitoring, attack surface change detection, Vigilar integration, 4-hour alert SLA.
  • Enterprise — All of Professional + supply chain monitoring, threat actor tracking, dedicated analyst, 1-hour alert SLA, monthly threat briefing.
